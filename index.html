Our Story

A touch of elegance,
a world of expertise

Touché Hair Services was born from a simple belief: that great hair has the power to transform not just how you look, but how you feel. Based in the heart of Caterham, Surrey, we've spent years building a reputation for exceptional colour work, precision cutting, and a warm, welcoming environment that keeps clients coming back.

Our name says it all. Touché — a nod to that perfect, well-placed stroke of genius. Whether it's a seamless balayage, a sharp cut, or a total colour transformation, we bring craft and care to every single chair.

What We Offer

Services crafted for you

From your first consultation to your final blow-dry, every service is tailored to complement your individual style, hair type, and lifestyle.

01

Hair Colour

From rich, dimensional balayage to bold fashion shades and seamless root blends — our colour specialists work with your natural tone to create something extraordinary.

02

Precision Cuts

Every cut begins with a thorough consultation. We take time to understand your lifestyle, face shape, and texture to deliver a style that works for you every day.

03

Highlights

Sunlit, natural, or statement — our highlighting techniques add depth, movement, and brightness that looks effortlessly beautiful in every light.

04

Treatments

Restore, nourish, and strengthen with our professional treatment range — from deep conditioning masks to keratin smoothing and bond repair therapies.

05

Styling

Blowdrys, updos, special occasion styling and everything in between. Leave our chair feeling polished and perfectly finished for any moment.

06

Extensions

Add length, volume, or both with expertly applied hair extensions that blend seamlessly — so natural, only you'll know they're there.
